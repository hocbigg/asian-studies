# Other curricula

This curriculum is one of a few efforts to inform learners how they can pursue Asian Studies independently. Below are a few other such efforts.

- [Asia for Educators (Columbia University)](https://afe.easia.columbia.edu/)
- [Harvard China Humanities Series (edX)](https://pll.harvard.edu/series/china-humanities)
- [National Consortium for Teaching about Asia (NCTA) Resources](https://www.ncta.osu.edu/)
